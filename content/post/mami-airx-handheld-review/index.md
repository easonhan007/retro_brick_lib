The video introduces the new handheld console AirX by Mami Technology, covering its design, specifications, and performance. The unboxing experience reveals the console's appearance, color options, and accessories such as the A2C charging cable. The AirX features a 5.5-inch 1080P display, with dimensions similar to the Switch Lite and a weight of 275 grams, indicating good size and weight management. Design elements include glossy D-pad buttons, Hall-effect joysticks, and buttons with RGB lighting. The device also includes dual speakers, a Type-C port, a 3.2mm headphone jack, and a 5000mAh battery supporting 5V 3.15W charging, running on Android 14.

In terms of performance, the AirX comes pre-installed with various emulators and a game launcher for game searching and performance monitoring. Users can adjust fan modes and view real-time performance data. The video demonstrates the device's sound quality and display characteristics, noting that the sound is clear without distortion and the LCD screen is flicker-free. Future videos plan to test gaming performance and conduct an internal inspection. Finally, the video encourages viewers to leave feedback in the comments and thanks them for their support and viewership.
