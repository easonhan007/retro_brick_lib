The iNEO Pocket Ace is a new handheld console featuring a 4.5-inch 1080p LCD screen with a 3:2 aspect ratio and a high resolution of 1620x1080. Its LCD screen eliminates flickering and offers a maximum brightness of 550 nits, making it ideal for outdoor use. The device is built with a matte-finish body available in multiple colors, providing comfortable ergonomics. Powered by a Snapdragon G3X G2 processor and running on Android 13, it supports performance, gaming, and streaming modes, offering robust performance.

In terms of design, the iNEO Pocket Ace features Hall-effect joysticks and conductive rubber buttons for excellent tactile feedback. The top micro-switch triggers allow for customizable functions, and the 0916B vibration motor provides strong feedback. The speakers are positioned at angled corners, delivering better audio quality compared to bottom-placed designs. Additional features include TF card expansion, video output, and a fingerprint scanner. With a 6000mAh battery and 40W fast charging, it delivers impressive battery life and quick recharging.

During gaming tests, the iNEO Pocket Ace smoothly runs high-performance emulator games like PS2 and GBA, maintaining stable frame rates. It supports high-quality Android games with optimized button mapping for enhanced gameplay. Furthermore, the device excels at low-latency PC streaming, with excellent decoding and responsiveness. Overall, the iNEO Pocket Ace is a versatile handheld console catering to retro gaming enthusiasts, balancing performance and portability.
