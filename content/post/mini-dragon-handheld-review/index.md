The MINILOONG handheld, priced at 299 RMB, stands out with its simple design and practical features. It is equipped with an [RK3566 processor](https://www.rock-chips.com/a/cn/product/RK35xilie/2021/0113/1273.html), 1GB RAM, and 8GB of storage (expandable). The device runs on a Linux-based system, offering high playability. The single joystick design supports pressing, with lighting effects around the joystick. The acrylic removable shell allows for customization, while the buttons use conductive rubber materials, providing good tactile feedback. The back of the console features anti-slip textures for better grip. It supports mini HDMI video output, Type-C charging, OTG, a 3.5mm headphone jack, and a single speaker.
