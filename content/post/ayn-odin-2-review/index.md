This video introduces the AYN Odin 2, a high-performance Android handheld gaming console powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, priced at just 1999 RMB. The Odin 2 continues the family design, featuring a more rounded and thicker body to accommodate a larger battery and better cooling system. Its ergonomic design ensures a comfortable grip.

The Odin 2 includes various ports, such as a TF card slot supporting up to 2TB storage, a mini HDMI output, and a fully functional Type-C port. It also features dual speakers and a 3.5mm headphone jack. The button setup consists of a tactile D-pad with dome switches, clickable analog sticks, and soft silicon ABXY buttons. R2 and L2 are linear trigger buttons for better gaming control.

In terms of performance, the Odin 2 is equipped with the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, offering exceptional graphical capabilities. The tested unit includes 12GB RAM and 256GB storage, while the top-tier variant provides 16GB RAM and 512GB storage. It runs on Android 13 and weighs 415.4 grams, making it relatively moderate in weight compared to devices like the Switch OLED or Steam Deck.
