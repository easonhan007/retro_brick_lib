# Review of Anil Pocket S2 Pro: The Powerful Android Handheld

The Anil Pocket S2 Pro is an advanced Android handheld device powered by Qualcomm's third-generation G3 processor, supporting 20W performance output. It features a 6.3-inch 2K LCD display, a 10,000mAh battery, and 60W PD fast charging, ensuring long-lasting battery life and quick charging. Compared to its predecessor, the S2 Pro is larger, thicker, and heavier at 440g, with rounded edges for improved grip comfort.

In terms of button layout, the S2 Pro introduces anti-slip textures, micro-motion shoulder buttons, and swappable eight-direction D-pad buttons, offering smoother operation and better ergonomics. Additional features include a 3.5mm headphone jack and relocated SD card slot for enhanced usability. Performance-wise, the S2 Pro scores an impressive 2.2 million in benchmarks, significantly outperforming the previous model. Its robust active cooling system maintains stable performance during PC gaming and high-demand emulation, consistently achieving 60 fps even in large-scale scenes and streaming modes.

For gaming, the S2 Pro supports controller mode and button mapping, providing seamless integration with Android games like Genshin Impact. Gameplay is smooth with precise controls and exceptional visuals. The teardown reveals high-density cooling fins and a large fan, showcasing the meticulous internal design. While its high price is a notable drawback, the S2 Pro delivers substantial upgrades in performance and gaming experience. It is an ideal choice for gamers seeking powerful and versatile handheld gaming solutions.
