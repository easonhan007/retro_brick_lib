# Tremio Smart: A Compact and Affordable Handheld Console

This video reviews the Tremio Smart handheld console released in 2022, highlighting its key features, performance, and user experience. Initially priced at 289 yuan, it now costs 165 yuan, offering significant value for money. Compact and portable, the console supports WiFi and Bluetooth for online multiplayer gaming and runs various emulator games, including arcade, GBA, GBC, NDS, and PS1 titles.

The Tremio Smart features a 2.4-inch IPS screen with 240p resolution and wide viewing angles. Its button layout uses tactile dome switches, offering high precision though the tactile feel may vary by user. Powered by an AM processor and 128MB of RAM, it handles PS1 and older games smoothly. Shortcut key combinations allow adjustments for volume, brightness, and acceleration, enhancing usability. It also includes quick save and load functions for convenient game progress management.

The video demonstrates the console's multiplayer compatibility, syncing seamlessly with the Tremio Smart Pro for cooperative gameplay. Weighing just 98.9 grams, the device is lightweight and ergonomically designed for easy portability. However, it has some drawbacks, such as the small screen size, a speaker placement that may be obstructed during use, and unnecessary lighting (which can be turned off).

Overall, the Tremio Smart provides a well-rounded feature set with excellent portability and entertainment value. While it lacks the power of higher-end devices, it’s an appealing choice for budget-conscious users who enjoy compact gaming on the go.
