The Anbernic RG477M is a premium retro handheld that excels in design, performance, and craftsmanship. It features a 1280×960 resolution 4:3 full-lamination 120Hz IPS display and an all-metal CNC aerospace-grade aluminum alloy body, offering exceptional durability and aesthetics. The button design includes hall-effect joysticks and conductive rubber buttons, providing a comfortable gaming experience with customizable RGB lighting. The shoulder buttons are compact and lightweight, perfect for portability.

Powered by the Dimensity 8300 processor, with performance comparable to the Snapdragon 8+, the RG477M supports various gaming scenarios, including PC emulation and Android AAA games. Tests show smooth gameplay, stable frame rates, and versatile resolution and button mapping options. Its 5300mAh battery supports fast charging and video output, with a well-designed cooling system featuring a rear fan and heat sink for optimal performance.

Compared to other handhelds, the RG477M stands out with its lightweight and slim design. The audio quality is clear but slightly low in volume. In gaming tests, it performs smoothly across different platforms, including GBA, NGC emulators, and PS2 games, with detailed visuals and low latency. It’s particularly suitable for retro and modern gaming enthusiasts. Overall, the RG477M combines high-quality craftsmanship and robust performance, making it an excellent choice for potential buyers.
