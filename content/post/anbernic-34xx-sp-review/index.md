Anbernic has unveiled its latest handheld console model, the 34XX SP, which stands out for its refined design and innovative features. The packaging includes three accessories: tempered glass, screen cleaning wipes, and a user manual, along with an A2C charging cable. The console is powered by the Allwinner H700 processor with 2GB RAM and features a 3.4-inch IPS full-laminated screen supporting point-to-point GBA resolution. The buttons are responsive, with the D-pad utilizing dome switches, while the recessed joysticks support press-down shortcut combinations.

The 34XX SP boasts a high-quality metal hinge with two fixed angles and Hall sensor functionality. Its design integrates volume control, TF card slots, Type-C charging port, HDMI output, and a headphone jack. The battery has a capacity of 3300mAh, providing up to 6 hours of playtime.

The console runs a Linux-based custom system, supporting functionalities like game emulation, e-books, music and video playback, and Bluetooth and WiFi connectivity for game streaming. It is compatible with platforms ranging from FC to PC and smoothly runs high-performance games such as Tekken 6 and Contra. The joystick and D-pad offer precise directional control, ensuring a detailed gaming experience.

Additionally, the 34XX SP excels in portability with its foldable design and magnetic protective case. Its internal structure is well-organized, featuring meticulous craftsmanship, excellent screen display quality, and comfortable hinge mechanisms. Overall, the 34XX SP strikes a balance between practicality and aesthetics, making it an ideal console for gaming enthusiasts.
