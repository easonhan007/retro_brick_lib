The Annual Pocket Vert is a meticulously designed handheld gaming console with a high-resolution screen of 1600x1440, supporting 10x point-to-point GB and GBC resolution for exceptional display clarity. Boasting a brightness of 450 nits and a 3.5-inch screen size, it features a CNC unibody metal design with beveled edges for a comfortable grip. Powered by the Snapdragon 8+ Gen1 processor, it delivers robust performance, supported by a 6000mAh battery and active cooling system for stable operation.

In terms of controls, the device replaces traditional physical joysticks with touch-sensitive ones, capable of L3 and R3 press functionality. It also doubles as a touchpad. The sidebar offers extensive options for quick adjustments, including volume, brightness, performance mode, and filter settings. Additionally, the console supports TF card expansion (up to 2TB), HD vibration feedback, and the return of the 3.5mm headphone jack.

The minimalist design comes in multiple color options (white, black, and red) and weighs 317g, offering a solid feel. The package includes replaceable shoulder buttons with anti-slip and glossy finishes. The screen delivers incredibly sharp visuals with a PPI of 615, making it ideal for gamers seeking high-quality graphics. The device also supports video output and Type-C fast charging (up to 45W), making it versatile.

Overall, the Annual Pocket Vert combines aesthetics, performance, and portability, making it a compelling choice for gaming enthusiasts and casual users alike.
